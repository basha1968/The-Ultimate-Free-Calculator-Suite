Sleep Science

Sleep occurs in cycles of approximately 90 minutes, each consisting of four stages:

  • N1: Light sleep (1-5 mins)
  • N2: Deeper sleep (10-60 mins)
  • N3: Deep sleep (20-40 mins)
  • REM: Dream sleep (10-60 mins)

Waking at the end of a cycle (during light sleep) helps you feel more refreshed.
