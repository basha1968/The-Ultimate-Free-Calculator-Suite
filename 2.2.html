Proper Measurement Guidelines

  • Height: Measure without shoes, heels together, looking straight ahead
  • Weight: Measure in minimal clothing, preferably in the morning
  • Neck: Measure below the larynx with tape sloping downward
  • Waist: Measure at the narrowest point (men) or at navel level (women)
  • All measurements should be taken twice and averaged for accuracy
