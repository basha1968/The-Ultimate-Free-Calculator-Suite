Understanding Your Cycle

Menstrual Cycle Phases:

  • Period: Days 1-5 (average) - shedding of uterine lining
  • Follicular Phase: Days 6-13 - preparation for ovulation
  • Ovulation: Around day 14 - release of egg from ovary
  • Luteal Phase: Days 15-28 - preparation for possible pregnancy

Fertile Window: The 5 days before ovulation and the day of ovulation are your most fertile days.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. All calculations are performed locally on your device. No data is sent to any server or stored anywhere.

This tool is GDPR compliant. We do not use any tracking cookies. Ad networks may use cookies with your consent.

Cycle Tracking Tips

  • Track your cycle for at least 3 months for better accuracy
  • Note symptoms like cramps, mood changes, and energy levels
  • Use basal body temperature for more precise ovulation prediction
  • Remember that stress can affect cycle length
