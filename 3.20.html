Mathematics Grade Calculator Help

This professional grade calculator helps you calculate your course grade using both weighted and unweighted systems.

Weighted System

Your overall grade is calculated by summing the products of each category's weight and average score:

Overall Grade = Σ(Weight × Average Score)

Where Σ represents the sum of all categories.

Unweighted System

Your overall grade is calculated as a simple percentage:

Overall Grade = (Points Earned ÷ Points Possible) × 100%

GPA Conversion

  • A: 90-100% = 4.0 GPA
  • B: 80-89% = 3.0 GPA
  • C: 70-79% = 2.0 GPA
  • D: 60-69% = 1.0 GPA
  • F: 0-59% = 0.0 GPA

Instructions:

  1. Select your grading system (weighted or unweighted)
  2. Enter your category details or total points
  3. Click "Calculate Grade" to see your results
  4. Export or share your results using the export options

Note: All calculations are performed locally in your browser for privacy and security.
