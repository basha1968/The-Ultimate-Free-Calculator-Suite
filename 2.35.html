Volume Formulas

Cube

Volume = side × side × side = side³

Sphere

Volume = (4/3) × π × radius³

Cylinder

Volume = π × radius² × height

Cone

Volume = (1/3) × π × radius² × height

Rectangular Prism

Volume = length × width × height

Pyramid

Volume = (1/3) × base area × height
