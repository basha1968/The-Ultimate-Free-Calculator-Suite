BTU Information

What is BTU?

British Thermal Unit (BTU) is a measurement of heat energy. One BTU is the amount of energy needed to raise the temperature of one pound of water by one degree Fahrenheit.

Sizing Guidelines

  • 150-250 BTU/sq ft: Average room
  • 300-350 BTU/sq ft: Poor insulation
  • 100-150 BTU/sq ft: Excellent insulation

Pro Tips

  • Add 10% for sunny rooms
  • Subtract 10% for shaded rooms
  • Size up for kitchens
  • Size down for bedrooms
