Percentage Tips

  • To find X% of a number, multiply the number by X/100
  • To calculate percentage increase: ((New - Original)/Original) × 100
  • Reverse percentages: Original = New / (1 + Percentage/100)
  • Use percentage points when comparing percentages
  • Compound percentages require sequential calculation
