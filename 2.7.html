What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic life-sustaining functions at rest.

BMR is the calories you burn at complete rest. This is your minimum calorie need.

TDEE is your total daily calorie expenditure including all activities.

To maintain weight, consume calories equal to your TDEE. To lose weight, consume less than your TDEE.

How to Use Your Results

Understanding your BMR and TDEE is essential for effective weight management:

  • Weight Maintenance: Consume calories equal to your TDEE
  • Weight Loss: Create a deficit of 500 calories/day to lose ~1 lb per week
  • Muscle Gain: Consume 250-500 calories above your TDEE while strength training

For best results, combine your calorie targets with a balanced diet and regular exercise.
