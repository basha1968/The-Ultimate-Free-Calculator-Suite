How to Use This Calculator ‚ñº

This calculator helps you organize your personal budget by tracking your income and expenses.

  • 1. Enter Income & Expenses: Use the "Add Income" and "Add Expense" buttons to add new rows. For each item, enter a name (e.g., "Salary", "Rent"), the amount, and its frequency (Monthly, Weekly, Annually, or One-time).
  • 2. Select Currency: Choose your preferred currency from the dropdown at the top.
  • 3. Calculate: Click the "Calculate Budget" button to see your monthly summary, detailed breakdowns, and visual charts.
  • 4. Review Results:
    • Total Monthly Income: Your total income normalized to a monthly figure.
    • Total Monthly Expenses: Your total expenses normalized to a monthly figure.
    • Net Monthly Income: Your income minus your expenses. A positive number means you have money left; negative means a deficit.
    • Monthly Savings/Deficit: Clearly states if you're saving or overspending.
  • 5. Export & Share: Use the buttons to print, save as PDF/Excel/CSV, or share your budget scenario with others via a unique link.
